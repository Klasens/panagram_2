Panagram

About

A unit of output: Panagrams measure the performer in extended, macro-sized cycles of work. Using periodization, quality theory, and GTD methodology, this technique seeks to maximize the value of deep work produced over intervals of months rather than minutes.

What Is A Panagram?

The Panagram is a span of time where the performer explicitly attempts to maximize deep work over a period of weeks to months.
